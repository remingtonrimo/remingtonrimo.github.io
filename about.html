Hey! My name is Remington Motte and I'm from Price, UT, but I have lived all over southern Utah. I attented Utah Valley University in Orem, UT, where I received my Bachelors of Science in Biology. During my tenure at UVU, I worked in the chemistry department where prepared the general and analytical chemistry labs and was a teachers assistant for Human Anatomy and General Chemistry (UVU directory here). In my junior year, I joined Dr. Hjelmen's research lab where I developed my own independent research project. In early 2023, I joined Dr. Brooks lab and have been variant genes associated with alcohol resistance. I love learning, and so I want to continue my education by obtaining my PhD. and eventually becoming a professor!

Experience
