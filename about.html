Hey! My name is Remington Motte and I'm from Price, UT, but I have lived all over southern Utah. I attented Utah Valley University in Orem, UT, where I received my Bachelors of Science in Biology. During my tenure at UVU, I worked in the chemistry department where prepared the general and analytical chemistry labs and was a teachers assistant for Human Anatomy and General Chemistry. In my junior year, I joined Dr. Hjelmen's research lab where I developed my own independent research project. Recently I joined Dr. Brooks lab and have been measuring the growth curve of different Staphylococcus species. I love learning, and so I want to continue my education by obtaining my PhD. and eventually becoming a professor!

Experience

While in Dr. Hjelmen's lab, I gained skills in phylogenetic reconstruction methods as well as R-studio for data visualization and analysis. I am currently learning about the methods used to analyze amino acid composition and searching for conserved and hypervariable regions of genes.


I joined Dr. Brooks lab in the begining of January of 2023. In her lab I have been gaining wet lab skills while investigating the growth rates of Staphylococcus species, as well their resistance to biocide.


From my other lab and work experiences I have gained skills in: preparing buffers and solutions, usage and implentation of chemical storages, navigating a supercomputer, bash scripting, linux fu, website design.
